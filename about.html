About LMS

LMS was started in 2024. Our shop was created because we love momos 🥟🥟🥟 and wanted to share them with everyone. We use fresh ingredients 🥬 to make momos that are super tasty 😋. Whether you want a quick snack 🍴 or a big meal 🥢, we want to make sure you have a great time ✨. We hope our shop becomes a fun and special place in our town where people can enjoy good food 🍽️, nice talks 🗨️, and happy moments ❤️.


Momos Trivia

When did the momos first apears in india?

A large number of Tibetans emigrated to India following the 1959 Tibetan uprising, bringing their recipes for momos with them.
